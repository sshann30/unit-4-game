Champions League

A Crystal Collector Game


Each of the 4 teams below will have a hidden random number assigned 0-12.

The value of each team is hidden from you until you click on it.

Click on the teams to accumulate numbers and match the "Random Number".

You win the game by matching your total score to the "Random Number".

If your total score goes above the random number, you lose.

The game will change all random values after every game.
